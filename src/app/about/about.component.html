About Me

I am currently employed at Financial Information Technologies LLC, aka "Fintech" as a Technical Product Owner. I have been with the company since March 2023 and have quickly grown into a confident Data Engineering role.

I oversee collect, ingest, process, harmonize, and publish workflows across our data platform—including over 150 data collection points spanning depletion, shipment, inventory, and invoice datasets.

I am a Fall 2022 graduate from the University of Miami with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science on the Data Science Track and a minor in Mathematics.

In 2021 and 2022 I worked as a Software Engineering summer intern at Thermo Fisher Scientific, gaining first-hand exposure to industry software development.

At the University of Miami I was Vice President of Recruitment for the Inter Fraternity Council, an AP Computer Science A Tutor in Java, and an active member of the Data Analytics Student Association.

Outside of work I enjoy collecting records, lifting, cooking, soccer, golf, skiing, traveling, and spending time with family and friends.

About Me

I'm a Technical Product Owner and Data Engineer at Financial Information Technologies (Fintech), where I've built and scaled a data platform that touches every corner of the beverage alcohol industry's supply chain.

Since joining in March 2023, I've grown from building ELT pipelines to owning the full product lifecycle — driving roadmap decisions, leading Agile ceremonies, and aligning engineering work to business outcomes across depletion, shipment, inventory, and invoice datasets.

I graduated Cum Laude from the University of Miami in December 2022 with a B.S. in Computer Science (Data Science Track) and a minor in Mathematics, earning a 3.85 GPA and six Provost's Honor Roll distinctions.

Before Fintech, I spent two summers at Thermo Fisher Scientific as a Software Engineering Intern, where I built production Java applications and got my first real taste of enterprise SDLC.

On campus I was VP of Recruitment for the Inter Fraternity Council, a Java tutor, and an active member of the Data Analytics Student Association.

Outside of work you'll find me collecting records, on the golf course, skiing, cooking, or spending time with family and friends.
